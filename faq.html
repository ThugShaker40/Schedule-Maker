Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to retype long activities?
Each time slot is independent for flexibility. We suggest using copy/paste or predictive fill to speed up repeating entries.
Can I assign colors to my schedule?
Yes! You can manually drag color swatches into cells. While it's not automatic, it helps visually separate activity types.
How do I repeat weekly or daily tasks?
We support autofill using your typing history. As you reuse entries, suggestions will appear to speed up filling similar tasks.
Can input be faster for frequent items?
Yes! As you type, the app suggests previous phrases. You can navigate them with your keyboard and auto-complete.
The mobile view scrolls sideways. Why?
We're actively improving mobile layout. For now, try landscape mode or zoom out to view more columns at once.
Will the site send me reminders?
To keep the site private and offline, we don’t use notifications. We recommend printing your schedule or using personal alarms.
Can I get alerts for upcoming tasks?
Alerts usually require a backend or account system. Since our site avoids accounts, reminders aren't supported for now.
Is it possible to view previous schedules?
Not currently. This site saves only to your browser, so there’s no cloud history. You can save schedules as images or PDFs.
